🕌 Prayer Timer — Privacy Policy

موقّت الصلاة · Gebetszeiten

Effective: April 2025

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Prayer Timer ("the App", developed by Hani Mashkawi) handles your information.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

The app works fully offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device's GPS/location service to determine your geographic coordinates. This is used solely to calculate the five daily Islamic prayer times for your precise location.

  • Your coordinates are used only on-device and are never stored beyond the calculation needed for the current session or scheduled notifications.
  • We do not collect, transmit, log, or share your location with any server, third party, or analytics service.

2. Permissions Used

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — used only to calculate prayer times based on your location.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — used only to notify users of prayer times and reminders.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM — used only to trigger notifications at exact prayer times.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — used only to restore scheduled notifications after device restart.

3. Data Storage

All data (last-known coordinates, calculation method, language preference, notification settings) is stored exclusively in the app's private local storage on your device using Flutter's SharedPreferences.

All data remains on your device and is not accessible by us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party SDKs that collect personal data. No Firebase, no AdMob, no Crashlytics, no analytics.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this policy applies equally to all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the new version at the same URL. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach out by email: hani.mashkawi@gmail.com
