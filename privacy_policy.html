🕌 Prayer Timer — Privacy Policy

موقّت الصلاة · Gebetszeiten

Effective: April 2025

Last updated: April 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Prayer Timer ("the App", developed by Hani Mashkawi) handles your information. Our core principle is simple: we collect nothing and send nothing.

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device's GPS/location service to determine your geographic coordinates. This is used solely to calculate the five daily Islamic prayer times for your precise location.

  • Your coordinates are used only on-device and are never stored beyond the calculation needed for the current session or the next seven days of scheduled notifications.
  • We do not collect, transmit, log, or share your location with any server, third party, or analytics service.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting services that send data off-device.

2. Permissions Used

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — obtain coordinates for prayer-time calculation.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — display adhan and advance reminders at scheduled times.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM — fire notifications at the precise prayer minute.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — reschedule notifications after a device reboot.

3. Data Storage

All data (last-known coordinates, calculation method, language preference, notification settings) is stored exclusively in the app's private local storage on your device using Flutter's SharedPreferences. Nothing leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party SDKs that collect personal data. No Firebase, no AdMob, no Crashlytics, no analytics.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this policy applies equally to all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the new version at the same URL. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach out by email: hani.mashkawi@gmail.com

Datenschutzerklärung — Gebetszeiten

Die App verwendet den GPS-/Standortdienst Ihres Geräts, um Ihre geografischen Koordinaten zu ermitteln. Dies dient ausschließlich der Berechnung der fünf täglichen islamischen Gebetszeiten.
